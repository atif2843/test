Type Of Boxes

 

Slotted type boxes

  • Slotted type boxes consist of basically one piece with stitched, tapped or glued manufacturers' joint and top and bottom flaps. They are shipped flat, ready to use and require closing using the flap provided.
 

Telescope type boxes

  • Telescope type boxes consist of more than piece and are characterized by a lid and / or bottom telescoping over the body of the boxes.
 

Folder type boxes

  • Folder type boxes usually consist of only one piece of board. The bottom of the box is hinged to form two or all side walls and the cover. The boxes can be set up without stitching or tapping. Locking tabs, handles, display panels, etc, can be incorporated in the design.
 

Ready glued cases

  • Ready glued cases consist of basically one piece, are shipped flat and ready to use by simple setting up.
 

Rigid type boxes

  • Rigid type boxes consist of two separate end pieces and a body and require stitching or a similar operation before they can be used.
 

Slide type boxes

  • Slide type boxes consist of several pieces of linear and sleeves sliding in different direction into each other. This group also include outside sleeves for other cases.
